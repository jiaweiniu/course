$$y(k)=-a_1y(k-1)-a_2(k-2)+v(k),\ k=1,2,...,N$$
~\\

$$=[a_1~~a_2]
\begin{bmatrix}
  -y(k-1)\\-y(k-2)
\end{bmatrix}
+v(k)
$$
~\\

$$=
\theta^T\varphi(k)+v(k)$$
~\\

よって：~\\
$$\theta^T=[a_1~~a_2]$$
~\\
$$\varphi(k)=
\begin{bmatrix}
  -y(k-1)\\-y(k-2)
\end{bmatrix}$$
~\\

まだ：~\\
$$\hat{\theta}=G_n^{-1}h_n$$
~\\

$$h_n=\dfrac{1}{N}\sum\limits_{k=1}^{N}
\begin{bmatrix}
  -y(k-1)\\-y(k-2)
\end{bmatrix}y(k)$$
~\\

$$G_n=\dfrac{1}{N}\sum\limits_{k=1}^{N}
\begin{bmatrix}
  -y(k-1)\\-y(k-2)
\end{bmatrix}[-y(k-1)~~-y(k-2)]]$$
\\　~\\

$$[y(k-2),y(k-1),...,y(k+4)],\ k=1,2,...,N$$\\

$$[y(k-2),y(k-1),...,y(k+4)] = [y(-1),y(0),...,y(5)] = [1,\ 1,\ 3,\ 3,\ 5,\ 8,\ 13]$$
$$(\ y(k)=y(k-1)+y(k-2),a1=-1,a2=-1 \ )$$\\
で合わせて７点を設定します\\　~\\

$g_n^{-1}=\begin{bmatrix}
